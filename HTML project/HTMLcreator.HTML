E-11


E-11 It was the military standard for stormtroopers in the empire. It can be used as a one handed blaster with the stock folded and a two handed
weapon with the stock extended. It weighs around 2.7 kilograms and can shoot 500 shots at a maximum of 300 meters. But it was most effective at 100
meters. When folded the stock could be used as foregrip.

DC-17


The DC-17 is a compact pistol used highly throught the clone wars. The DC-17 was commonly used amoung high rankiing clones such as: Captains, Arc troopers,
and some commanders. It was a small compact heavy pistol that weighs in at about 1 kilogram with single fire rounds.

DL-44

procailmed "greatest pilot in the galaxy". The DL-44 is a heavy blaster effective at 50-75 meters. It is equip with a stun setting and is a single shot fire
rate.
