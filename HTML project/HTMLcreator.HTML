Early Life

Dorothy. In the late 50s George and his family moved to a racnch-style house. It covered 13 acres and had several walnut trees.
It was on the ranch where George discovered his intrest in cars and other motor vehicals. Later on in life he would become the
head of Allen Grants pit crew at the Grand PrixAt the age of 18 while driving his Fiat Bianchina crashed into a tree and reguired
to be put in a hospital. After this crash he was never able to race again. He graduated fromModesto jumior college in 1964 and he
enrolled to film school at the University of Southern California.

Where is he now?

After selling Star Wars to Disney for $4.1 billion George retired from film making. He currently lives in San Anselmo, California.
He would put most of the money from the deal towards his family philanthropy foundation where they have over $1 billion in assests.
